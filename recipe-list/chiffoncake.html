Chiffon Cake

Desciption

Simple, yet this fluffy cake is perfect for any and all ocassions!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sifted cake flour
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 7 large eggs, separated, divided
  • 3/4 cup cold water
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cream tartar

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Wash a 10-inch angel food tube pan in hot soapy water to ensure it is grease-free and well dried
  2. Sift flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt into a bowl. Add egg yolks, water, oil, vanilla extract, and lemon extract respectively into the center.
  3. Beat egg whites and the cream of tartar in a separate, large bowl until peaks are very stiff.
  4. Then beat the egg yolk batter until smooth and light. Pour slowly into egg whites. Gently fold the mixtures together with a spatula. Pour batter into angel food tub pan.
  5. Bake cake in the preheated oven for 55 minutes. Increase heat to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and continue baking until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 10 to 15 more minutes.
  6. Invert the pan onto a wire rack. Let cool completely before unmolding and frosting as desired.
